ABOUT OUR TOWN

Our Town was founded by four friends at a crossroad in their life in 1999. Tired of the modern comforts of city life, they decided to rough it in the country in order to impress their soon to be girlfriends who claimed all they ever wanted growing up was a pony.

We're glad they chose this spot of land because today, despite never buying horses for their now ex-wives, Our Town is a beautiful scenic reminder of the days before sky scrapers.

"I would never go back to living in the big city, but I do miss the opera."

Founder: Robert Hopscotch
